“What I found was really great enthusiasm for him”—Nigeria’s outsider candidate leads

Also on the daily podcast: the economic illiteracy of Myanmar’s junta and the bravery of a Soviet-era missionary

Oct 11th 2022

OUR CORRESPONDENT meets with Peter Obi, who has a handsome poll lead and an appeal that spans the country’s religions and ethnicities. But his presidential bid still faces obstacles. Myanmar’s ruling junta is doing more than suppressing the country’s people: it is battering the economy equally efficiently. And remembering Brother Andrew, who made daring deliveries behind the Iron Curtain. Runtime: 25 min
